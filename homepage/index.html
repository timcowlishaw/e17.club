It's a place for Walthamstow locals to learn about computing and the internet,
show off their creativity, and make friends.

It's loosely based on the ideas behind tilde.club, but with an added pinch of localism.

E17 club has two rules:
  - Be nice to each other
    (This means:
      - No hacking or attempting to hack other users accounts, and
      - Abide by the Geek Feminism Anti-Harrasment policy)
  - Don't do anything illegal.

E17 club is overseen by Tim Cowlishaw [email],
please get in touch with him with any questions or requests, or if you'd like to join!

Have fun!
